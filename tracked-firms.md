# State of Crypto Marketing 2026 — Tracked Firms

> **Replaces target-list.md (deprecated 2026-05-04).** These are the ~30 firms whose public marketing signals form the report's primary corpus. Tracked continuously through August 31, 2026. Selection criteria below.

## Selection criteria

A firm enters the tracked panel if it meets both of:
1. **Operates a regulated or about-to-be-regulated crypto-marketing function** at meaningful scale (i.e., not a token-launch project; the function has senior named leadership and a paid-media surface > $500K/yr).
2. **Has shipped enough public signal in the last 12 months** to support multi-theme synthesis (job postings + at least one of: press release, conference appearance, named agency relationship, public regulator interaction).

Firms that meet criterion 1 but produce minimal public signal are flagged in a separate "absence panel" — their lack of public signal is itself a data point under Theme 4 (MiCA readiness) and Theme 1 (gate-stack visibility).

## Workflow

- **Weekly corpus update:** every Friday, scan each tracked firm for new job postings, new senior hires/exits, new agency announcements, new public statements. Append to corpus.
- **Monthly absence audit:** which firms produced zero public signal in the past month — flag in `./findings/absence-panel.md`.
- **Inbound-nomination intake:** signals nominated via `hello@northpoint.fi` get evaluated against the corpus criteria. If they fit, they enter the corpus and the source is acknowledged in the report's appendix.
